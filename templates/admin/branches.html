How to Get GPS Coordinates

  1. Open Google Maps on your phone or computer
  2. Navigate to your branch location
  3. Right-click (or long-press on mobile) on the exact location
  4. Copy the coordinates that appear (e.g., 14.5995, 120.9842)
  5. Enter the first number as Latitude, second as Longitude
